Closing Remarks


This webpage has truly consumed a significant amount of effort and determination. Even though the requirements only called for a simple homepage, I pushed myself to go above and beyond to create something I’m genuinely satisfied with and proud of. I suppose that’s just who I am — if it doesn’t align with my vision, I won’t even begin it. Otherwise, there is no point at all, don't you agree?

The task was indeed challenging, especially when you know that there are countless freely available content management systems that allow people to publish dynamic websites (this web page is sadly static but I don't plan on being involved in web design anyway) without ever touching a line of code. It makes sitting down and building a webpage from scratch (even with the help of powerful frameworks like Bootstrap or fantastic tools like ChatGPT) feel particularly painstaking. But I’m glad I saw it through, and the experience I gained in the process (about HTML, CSS, JavaScript and Responsive Web Design) was, in the end, worth it — well, somewhat worth it ;-;.

I hope this project impresses those who visit it, especially the esteemed reviewers of my lab application :))). It will surely sooth the regrets that have been bothering me.

Oh, and maybe you would want to change the screen size to see the Responsive Design of this web page. Maybe you would then appreciate this more.

Thank you for your time!
